1. Masam Masam Manis



Masam Masam Manis is one of my favourite movie because i used to watch it with my late father when i was child.
We used to laugh together when we watched this movie and it is a part of a best memory for me and my dad.

Synopsis:

Masam Masam Manis (English: Sweet and Sour) is a 1965 Malaysian comedy film directed by and starring P. Ramlee.
The story takes place in Kuala Lumpur in 1965. This story is about a teacher, Sha'ari who is also a musician at night and Norkiah,
a young woman who rent the room next to his. Usually, Sha'ari and Norkiah worked at the nightclub with the latter with her friend,
Mariani, who is also a dancer. Both were frequently quarrel about their matters and annoys each other,
with one scene Norkiah flew the portrait to the wrong person instead of Sha'ari,
causing a massive fighting between the room occupants.
